## 正文

Valley里是单行线，并且不好停车，错过景点就要绕一大圈回来，堵车时太坑。所以我汇总了各风景点的比较好的停车位置（图1）：
	
[一R]37°42'58.5"N 119°39'58.1"W
景点：Pohono Bridge
	
[二R] Bridalveil Falls Trailhead
景点：Bridalveil Fall 图2
	
[三R] Cathedral Beach Picnic Area, TUOLUMNE MEADOWS, CA 95389
景点：Cathedral Beach 图3
	
[四R]Sentinel Beach Picnic Area, Yosemite National Park, Wawona, CA 95389
景点：Sentinel Beach
	
[五R] 37°44'10.4"N 119°35'58.0"W (路左侧趴车位)
景点：Swinging Bridge 图4-6
	
[六R] Yosemite Valley Chapel, 9000 Southside Dr, Yosemite Valley, CA 95389
景点：Yosemite Valley Chapel 图7
	
[七R] 37°44'38.6"N 119°35'23.8"W （小停车场，只能从Southside Dr进，开出只能去Northside Dr方向）；或者开到 Yosemite Village Parking, Yosemite Valley, CA 95389（大停车场，之后开去northside或者southside都可）
可以看两个景点：
Cook’s Meadow trail 图8-9
Sentinel Bridge 图10-11
	
[八R]37°44'44.5"N 119°35'49.3”W：左手边有parking的入口。
景点：Lower Yosemite Fall 图12
	
[九R] El Capitan Meadow, California 95389 (路左平趴趴车位)
景点：El Cap Meadow 图13-14
	
[一R][零R] 37°43'02.4"N 119°39'42.9"W (路左侧小停车场)
景点：Valley View 图15
